Decorating lives

Used for both decorative purpose and as medicine

Butterfly Blue Pea or Asian Piegeon Wings are used as medicinal herbs for a long time. The flowering plant spreads in vines which decorates the pathway in a eye soothing manner. Whereas the medicinal value of this flower keeps it at top of it's peers for soothing us internally. In true sense it decorates our life externally and internally

Different Usage

Herbal Tea

Like different floral flavors of tea such as chamomile and jasmine, bluebellvine or ternatea also has it's own flavored tea. Due to the beautiful color and medicinal value many people use the flowers as subsidiary of tea

Refreshment Drinks

For it's very charming blue pigmentation the blue flowers are first plucked and dried to be used as pigmentation for different drinks. The dried flowers are later stored in ice and then used for their beautiful color and flavor. The drinks are used wide spreadly and are refreshing in a soothing way
